About the Workshop

The Indo-German Workshop on Plant–Microbe Interactions: Implications for Plant Health and Their Interconnections will be held on October 27–28, 2025, at BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus, Rajasthan, India. This two-day event will bring together leading scientists, early-career researchers, and students from India and Germany to share the latest advances in plant–microbe interactions, plant immunity, plant–herbivore interactions, and sustainable agriculture. The program includes plenary and invited talks, thematic sessions, poster presentations, and student talks, offering a rich platform for scientific exchange and collaboration. With focused discussions on topics such as microbial infection strategies, chemical signalling, host immune responses, and symbiosis, this workshop aims to foster knowledge-sharing, highlight cutting-edge research, and strengthen Indo-German research networks in plant sciences.

How to Reach Pilani

By Air: The nearest airports are in Delhi (approx. 220 km) and Jaipur (approx. 235 km). Both airports are well-connected to major cities. From there, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Pilani.

By Train: The nearest major railway stations are Loharu (approx. 26 km) and Chirawa (approx. 16 km). Loharu is well-connected to Delhi. From these stations, you can easily find taxis and local buses to BITS Pilani.

By Road: Pilani is well-connected by road. Regular bus services are available from major cities like Delhi (from ISBT Kashmiri Gate) and Jaipur (from Sindhi Camp Bus Stand). Taxis are also readily available.
